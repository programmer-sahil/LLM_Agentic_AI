##  Overall Aim
This thesis investigates the rapid evolution of **Large Language Models (LLMs)**—from early transformer architectures to emerging **agentic AI frameworks**—that enable reasoning, planning, and tool use in complex automation tasks.  
It bridges **academic theory** and **practical experimentation**, providing insights valuable to **educators, developers, and small enterprises** adopting open-source AI technologies.
